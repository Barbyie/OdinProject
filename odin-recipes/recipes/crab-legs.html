Crab Legs

Crab meat is used in many cuisines around the world.

Discover a quick and delightful crab legs recipe featuring a flavorful garlicky butter sauce, ready in just 20 minutes! When a sale on snow crab legs at the market sparked my culinary inspiration, I sought a departure from the usual steamed, boiled, or grilled preparations. The result? A delectable crab leg dish crafted by combining various ingredients for a truly awesome flavor profile.

Transform your seafood dining routine with this easy-to-follow recipe that elevates crab legs to a whole new level of culinary excellence. The garlicky butter sauce adds a rich and savory dimension, complementing the sweetness of the crab legs. Whether you're a seafood enthusiast or just looking to try something new, this dish promises a delightful burst of flavors, making it an ideal choice for a quick and impressive meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Snow Crab clusters, thawed if necessary
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 1/8 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Cut a slit, length-wise, into the shell of each piece of crab.

  2. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until translucent, 1 to 2 minutes. Stir in parsley, pepper, and salt. Continue to heat mixture until bubbling.

  3. Add crab legs to the skillet; toss to coat with butter mixture. Simmer until completely heated through, 5 to 6 minutes.

  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
